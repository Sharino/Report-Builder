• 1General Information

In order to create your component you have to specify it's general information.

Title

Simple yet effective name to identify your component.

Required Title must be specified.

Note Title should be no less than 3 and no more than 30 symbols in length.

Type

The type of your component.

Required Type must be specified.

    All component Types:
  • KPI Displays selected Key Performance Indicators for selected metric values.
  • Table Displays a table for selected metric and dimension values.
  • Timeline Displays a timeline for selected metric and dimension values.
  • Chart Displays a chart for selected metric and dimension values.

  • 2Metrics

Metrics are individual elements of a dimension that can be measured as a sum or a ratio.

Required At least one metric is required to create a component.

Adding a metric

To add metric simply click the button in the list. Then find your desired metric in dropdown list or use search to access it faster.

Repositioning a metric

To reposition a metric click on handle icon () left to the metric element and drag and drop it to your desired position.

Note While dragging a gray placeholder will appear in the position where your metric would go.

Removing a metric

To remove a selected metric click icon on the right of the metric.

  • 3Dimensions

A dimension is a group of attributes that represent an area of interest.

Required At least one dimension is required for Table, Timeline and Chart Component types.

Adding a dimensions

To add dimension simply click the button in the list. Then find your desired dimension in dropdown list or use search to access it faster.

Repositioning a dimension

To reposition a dimension click on handle icon () left to the dimension element and drag and drop it to your desired position.

Note While dragging a gray placeholder will appear in the position where your dimension would go.

Removing a dimension

To remove a selected dimension click icon on the right of the dimension.
